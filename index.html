We are the project RESUME (cybersecurity studies in unconfined communications systems): project dedicated to strengthening the security of unconfined communications. We investigate and validate solutions for real-world scenarios involving drones (UAS) and electrical power systems, integrating field and laboratory testing, vulnerability mapping, and artificial intelligence-based protection strategies.
